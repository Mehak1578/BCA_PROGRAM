Welcome to the BCA Program

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at Eternal University is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in computer science and information technology. Our program is focused on practical and theoretical knowledge, preparing students for successful careers in the IT industry.

BCA is a three-year degree program. This degree is for those who want to study computer science, software engineering, information technology, information security, and networking technology. Candidates who have passed 10+2 examination from any of recognized board with minimum 45% Marks.

About the Program

The BCA program at Eternal University is a three-year undergraduate degree course that offers in-depth knowledge of computer applications, software development, and IT management. Our curriculum is designed to meet the needs of the rapidly evolving technology sector.

Curriculum

Our curriculum includes courses on programming languages, database management, web development, networking, and more. Students also have the opportunity to work on real-world projects and internships.

Admissions

Admissions to the BCA program are open for the upcoming academic session. Interested candidates can apply online through our university portal. For more information on eligibility criteria and the application process, please visit our admissions page.
